## 🚀 About The Project

**Guess The Words Game** is not just another word game—it's a global, innovative, and feature-rich experience that connects word lovers from all over the world! Challenge your mind, compete internationally, and expand your vocabulary while having fun.

---

## 🌐 Why Guess The Words Game is World-Class

- **Multi-language Support:** Play in English, Arabic, French, Spanish, and more!
- **International Leaderboards:** Compete with players globally—see your ranking in real time.
- **Cloud Sync:** Never lose progress; your data is always safe across devices.
- **Community Events:** Take part in tournaments, seasonal events, and special challenges.
- **Smart Suggestions:** Adaptive hints powered by AI.
- **Accessibility:** Designed for everyone, including colorblind and visually impaired users.
- **Continuous Updates:** New words, features, and enhancements released regularly.

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Prerequisites

- **Android Studio** Arctic Fox (or newer)
- **JDK** 11+
- **Minimum SDK:** 23 (Android 6.0)
- **Target SDK:** 33 (Android 13)

### Installation

```bash
git clone https://github.com/Ahmed01088/Guess_The_Words_Game.git
```
1. Open the project in **Android Studio**
2. Sync Gradle files
3. Run on your emulator or device

---

## 🎮 How to Play

1. **Choose a Mode:** Classic, Time Challenge, Practice, or join a live event.
2. **Guess the Words:** Use hints and your knowledge to reveal hidden words.
3. **Score High:** Earn bonus points for speed, streaks, and difficulty.
4. **Climb the Ranks:** Check your progress on global and local leaderboards.
5. **Connect & Compete:** Invite friends, join tournaments, and become a word master!

---

## 📝 Game Modes

| Mode              | Description                                              |
|-------------------|---------------------------------------------------------|
| **Classic**       | Unlimited time, lives-based, pure fun                   |
| **Time Challenge**| Beat the clock for extra rewards                        |
| **Practice**      | Unlimited tries, no pressure, perfect for learning      |
| **Daily Quests**  | New puzzles every day                                   |
| **Tournaments**   | Compete with players around the world (live events)     |
